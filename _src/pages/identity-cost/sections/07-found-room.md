  <p class="section-label reveal">Found Room</p>
  <div class="sidebar-section-content reveal" markdown="1">

The Identity Cost is a body of work developed by Daniel Fox over years of observation and lived experience — consulting with founders, watching the pattern repeat, and confronting it in himself.

Found Room is not a program built around this framework. It's a community that uses it as one available lens. Members encounter it not as a curriculum but as a language — a way of seeing what's often invisible inside a business and a life.

The full manuscript is in development. In the meantime, Found Room is the place to explore these ideas in community with founders who are living them.
