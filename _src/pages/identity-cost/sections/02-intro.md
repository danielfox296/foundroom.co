    <p class="italic-large">Every founder routes something personal through their business. Not intentionally. Not obviously. But over time, the business becomes the mechanism through which a founder answers questions that have nothing to do with the business.</p>
    <p class="italic-mid">Who am I? Do I matter? Am I good enough? Am I on the right path?</p>
    <p class="closing-line">The business answers these questions imperfectly, temporarily, and at significant cost to both the founder and the organization. This is The Identity Cost.</p>
