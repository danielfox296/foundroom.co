Find the others.

This is where founders who have been through it — or are in it — find each other. Not a network for deal flow. Not an accountability group. Just people who understand the specific kind of disorientation that comes with building something real.

Timothy Leary said "find the others." That's the whole idea.

There are founders who've been through an exit and found the aftermath disorienting. There are founders still running something who've lost the thread. Neither group fits the existing rooms — the optimization masterminds, the wealth management networks, the accountability groups. This is not that.

Free membership means you're in the room. Daniel reads every submission. If it feels like a fit, he'll reach out directly. No funnel. No pitch sequence. The room itself is conversations, not content — a space where the peers are actually your peers.
